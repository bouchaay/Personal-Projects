English

English is the most widely spoken language in the world. It is the official language of 53 countries and is spoken by over 1.5 billion people. It is the language of business, science, technology, and diplomacy. It is the language of the internet, and the language of the United Nations. It is the language of the world.
In our English classes, we primarily focus on preparing for careers. In the first year, we concentrate on developing our communication skills through various means. Specifically, we learn how to create a professional resume, how to effectively chair and take minutes at a meeting, and how to engage in professional interactions. These skills are essential for anyone pursuing a successful career and we will work diligently to ensure our mastery of them.
